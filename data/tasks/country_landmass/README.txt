TASK: Given the outline and name of a country, guess the landmass of the country.

DESCRIPTION. You will be shown 20 images outlining the shape of a country 
             and asked to guess the landmass of the country it corresponds to 
			 (units in sq km).

CORPUS: The complete set of country names was extracted from:
	http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_countries_and_dependencies_by_area
		
	A set of images was collected from the following websites. 
        The corpus was chosen as it has a standardized set of images 
	which ensures consistency across the 20 tasks.
	http://www.jetpunk.com/quizzes/guess-the-country-shape.php
	http://www.jetpunk.com/quizzes/guess-the-country-shape-2.php
	http://www.jetpunk.com/quizzes/guess-the-country-shape-3.php
	http://www.jetpunk.com/quizzes/guess-the-country-shape-4.php
	http://www.jetpunk.com/quizzes/guess-the-country-shape-5.php

		
METHODOLOGY: 
	TASKS
	A random sample of 20 countries was chosen from the corpus.
	
	ANSWER
	For each task, random sample of 1 country was chosen 
	from the complete list of countries.
	A point estimate answer of units sq km needs to be provided.
