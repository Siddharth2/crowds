TASK: Given the logo of a product of two different brand, guess which is the most popular one.

DESCRIPTION. You will be shown 2 images of a product of different brands.You have to guess the most popular one.

CORPUS: The random set of 20 countries was extracted from:
        http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Asian_countries_by_GDP_per_capita
        http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_North_American_countries_by_GDP_(PPP)
        http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_South_American_countries_by_GDP_(PPP)
        http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_African_countries_by_GDP_(PPP)
        http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_sovereign_states_in_Europe_by_GDP_(PPP)_per_capita
        
        The complete set of brand names was extracted from:
	http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Category:Brands_by_country
		
	A set of  images was collected from the following websites. 
        http://www.brandprofiles.com/miscellaneous-logos?p=5

	http://en.wikipedia.org/ 
		
        The popularity was measured using https://www.google.co.in/trends/
		
METHODOLOGY: 
	TASKS
	A random sample of 20 brands was chosen from the corpus. The random set of countries was selected from the corpus.
        Their popularity in the particular country was measured using https://www.google.co.in/trends/. The corresponding images was collected.
        
	
	ANSWERS
	For each task, the correct answer was obtained from  https://www.google.co.in/trends/. 
        A competing brand was chosen randomly from the set.
