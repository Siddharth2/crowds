TASK: Estimate the crime rate for states in the USA in 2013.

CORPUS: The most recent (2013) FBI uniform crime report, Table 5.
        Link: http://www.fbi.gov/about-us/cjis/ucr/crime-in-the-u.s/2013/crime-in-the-u.s.-2013/tables/5tabledatadecpdf/table_5_crime_in_the_united_states_by_state_2013.xls


METHODOLOGY: 20 states were sampled at random.

DESCRIPTION:  You will be given a list of 20 US states and asked to estimate
              the number of violent crimes per 100,000 inhabitants in 2013. 
              Violent crime is defined to include the offenses of murder, rape, robbery, and aggravated assault.
