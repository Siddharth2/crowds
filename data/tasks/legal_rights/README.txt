Task: Test of knowledge of legal rights in the US.

Detailed Description shown to participants:  

You will be asked 20 questions regarding civil rights in the United States relating to
privacy and police encouters.

Corpus: 
Questions were complied from existing online quizzes:
(1) https://www.eff.org/pages/know-your-digital-rights-quiz
(2) http://www.berkeleycopwatch.org/resources/KYR_Quiz_and_Key.pdf


Answer Type: Multiple Choice


Methodology: 
All questions from corpus (1) were used. The True/False questions from corpus (2) were transformed into multiple choice
questions to be consistent with the questions from corpus (1).
