
Task:  Estimate the energy consumption for the electric appliance.

Detailed Description shown to participants:  

“We will give you the names of a series of electronic appliances. 
For each question, you will be asked to estimate the average energy consumption 
(based on average use).


Corpus: http://siliconvalleypower.com/index.aspx?page=1922


Methodology: 
The list was restricted to common household appliances. Medical equipment, hot tup 
appliances were first removed from the list. For each category (kitchen, heating, laundry, lighting, etc.),
sample a maximum of 2 appliances were chosen at random.

Answer Type: point estimate (float)
